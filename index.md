**<u>Recent Procedure Updates</u>** <sup>Last Updated: 04/11/2025</sup>  

- Do not use 510w panels unless it is necessary. Hard to install, heavy and bulky.
- Do not sell DC Coupling to customers unless the customer falls under one the following:
  - <u>NEW</u> Panel & Battery install;
  - Customer Panels are installed after 2023 and the cables are 4mm (1500v)
  - QLD where GSD requirements are applicable.
- Australian Panel cabling requirements changed in 2023. The old standard requires 2.5mm cables (1,000V), while the new standard requires 4mm cables (1,500V). For panels installed before 2023, we may have to replace all cablings connecting their panels to the inverter to be within these requirements.
- Removal costs have changed to the following:
  - Old Panel Removal: $50 per panel
  - Old Inverter disconnect & removal: $300
  - Old battery disconnect & removal: $500
  - **Customer must dispose of the removed panels/inverter/battery themselves. Installers do not take these items with them.**
- **Do not sell customers the Basic (H1-5.0) FOXESS Inverters, we do not have any in stock, all customers that have been quoted Basic Packages from this point onwards will need to have their contracts amended.**
- Prices will be increasing across the board from November 10th, We will no longer offer any blackout protection for free, as blackout protection now costs $300 per circuit. [see here for more info.](price-increases)
